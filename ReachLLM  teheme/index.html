FAQs

Have Questions?
Here's what we hear often

What is Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)?

GEO is the practice of optimizing content and brand presence for AI-powered search engines and chatbots. Unlike traditional SEO that focuses on ranking in search results, GEO ensures your brand appears naturally in AI conversations and responses when users ask relevant questions.

How does ReachLLM measure AI visiblity?

ReachLLM tests your brand across multiple large language models including ChatGPT, Gemini, DeepSeek, Claude, Perplexity, and Grok using real customer scenarios. We provide comprehensive competitive analysis showing which brands appear for your target prompts. Our GEO Audit reveals your website's specific strengths and weaknesses for AI visibility, while Prompt Explorer lets you test specific prompts to see exactly where your brand stands compared to competitors.

What makes GEO different from traditional SEO?

Traditional SEO optimizes for Google's algorithm, but GEO is conversational and context-driven. Our platform generates customer conversation prompts that naturally lead AI platforms to recommend your brand, focusing on how AI understands and presents your value proposition.

How quickly can I see insigts?

You'll get immediate insights from our AI analysis. Brand Intelligence results are available within minutes, showing current AI visibility and providing actionable prompts you can implement right away.

Which AI platforms do you cover?

We currently analyze ChatGPT, Google Gemini, DeepSeek, Claude, Perplexity, and Grok, providing comprehensive coverage of the AI search landscape. Our multi-platform approach ensures you understand how your brand appears across all major AI platforms.

What language do you support?

ReachLLM supports 29 different languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Chinese, Arabic, Bengali, and more, allowing you to test and optimize your brand's AI visibility across global markets. You can run Brand Intelligence, Prompt Explorer, and GEO Audit in your target languages to understand how AI platforms present your brand to international audiences.

Does ReachLLM work for local businesses and regional markets?

Absolutely! ReachLLM provides country-specific analysis tailored for local brands and regional businesses. Our platform analyzes your competitors within your specific country context, generates location-relevant prompts and keywords, and tests how AI platforms respond to queries in your local market. Whether you're a local restaurant, regional service provider, or country-specific brand, we ensure your AI optimization strategy is relevant to your geographic market and local customer base.

What is LLMS.txt and Why it is important?

LLMS.txt is a special file that helps AI platforms better understand your website content and brand information. It's like a roadmap for AI systems to find and present your most important information accurately. Our AI Generator automatically creates optimized LLMS.txt files for your website, ensuring AI platforms have the right context about your brand when responding to user queries.

How does the AI Generator help with content optimization?

Our AI Generator analyzes your website and automatically creates LLM-optimized content. It generates LLMS.txt files and provides page-specific content recommendations to improve your AI visibility. We're currently beta testing advanced features that create LLM-optimized content for individual pages, helping with AI-focused copywriting and content strategy.

Do I need tecnical experties for GEO?

Not at all! ReachLLM is designed for marketers, brand managers, and business owners. Our AI generates ready-to-use customer conversation prompts and provides clear, actionable recommendations that your team can implement immediately without any technical knowledge.
